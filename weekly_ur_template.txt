II. ASAM CRITERIA (provide a few sentences that describe the ASAM dimensions)

A. Biomedical Conditions & Complications (Dimension 2)
Other than withdrawal, are there physical illnesses, medical reasons, or chronic conditions that will complicate or affect treatment?

B. Emotional, Behavioral or Cognitive Conditions and Complications (Dimension 3)
Are there current psychiatric, psychological, behavioral, emotional, or cognitive problems that complicate treatment? Are there chronic conditions that affect treatment? Are these emotional, behavioral, or cognitive problems related to the addiction, or are they autonomous? Do they warrant specific mental health treatment?

C. Readiness to Change (Dimension 4)
Is the individual aware of a need for change? What is the individual's level of commitment to and readiness for change? To what degree is the individual compliant with treatment? Are they aware of the relationship between substance use and negative consequences?

D. Relapse Problem Potential (Dimension 5)
Does the individual possess understanding and skills with which to cope with addictive or mental disorders to prevent relapse, continued use, or continued problems such as suicidal behavior? How severe are the problems that may continue or reappear if the individual is not successfully engaged in treatment at this time? Is the individual aware of relapse triggers or ways to cope with cravings? Do they have impulse control skills to address impulses to "use" or to harm self or other? Is the individual in immediate danger of continued severe mental health distress or alcohol/drug use?

E. Recovery Environment (Dimension 6)
Do family members, significant others, living situations, work/school pose a threat to safety or individual's participation in treatment? Does the individual have supportive friendships, financial resources, educational/vocational resources that increase the likelihood of successful treatment? Are there legal, vocational, social service agency, or criminal justice mandates that may motivate the individual to engage in treatment? Does the individual have transportation, housing, employment, or child care issues that need to be addressed?
